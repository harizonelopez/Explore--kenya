THE BOOKING GUIDE

It contains fields for the destination, cost, date, time, phone number, and preference.
The preference field is a dropdown that allows users to select whether they want to book as an individual or as a group/family.
If the user selects group/family, an additional field is displayed for the number of people involved.
The form also contains a submit button that allows users to submit their booking request.

  • Destination: Enter the destination of your choice.
  • Cost: Enter the cost of the trip.
  • Date: Enter the date of the trip.
  • Time: Enter the time of the trip.
  • Phone Number: Enter your phone number.
  • Select Your Preference: Choose whether you want to book as an individual or as a group/family.
  • If Group/Family: Enter the number of people involved.
  • Click the "Book" button to submit your booking request.
