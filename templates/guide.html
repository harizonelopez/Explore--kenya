User Guide

Welcome to Travel Kenya!

Our platform is designed to make your travel planning as easy and convenient as possible. Here's how you can navigate and use the website effectively:

Booking Your Adventure

Follow these steps to complete your booking:

  • Destination: Enter the destination you wish to visit. You can select from the list of popular destinations or enter a custom location.
  • Cost: Input the cost of the trip. This helps in budgeting and planning your adventure.
  • Date: Choose the date of your trip using the date picker. Ensure you select a suitable date for your convenience.
  • Time: Specify the time you would like to begin your adventure.
  • Phone Number: Provide a valid contact number. This is necessary for confirmation and communication purposes.
  • Select Your Preference:
    • Individual: Book as a single traveler.
    • Group/Family: If you are booking for multiple people, select this option. An additional field will appear for you to specify the number of people involved.
  • Once all fields are completed, click on the "Book" button to submit your booking request.

Navigating the Website

  • Use the Menu at the top of the page to explore different sections of the website such as Home, Destinations, About Us, and Contact Us.
  • Check out our Adventure Guide section for travel tips and popular travel packages.
  • If you need any help, visit the FAQ or reach out through the Contact Us page.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have further questions, feel free to Contact Us. We are here to assist you and make your travel experience memorable!
